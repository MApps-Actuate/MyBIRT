Drag components from the bar on the left into the area on the right to create your layout. You can drop components over existing items in the report or drop them on the blank striped areas to insert. You can delete components by dragging in an Empty Slot or by clicking the popup . Some components may have further configurable settings accessible via the popup .

You can change the master page to set the number of columns and other common themes that apply to the whole page. You can also insert a page break and add a new section.

A draft copy of your design is automatically saved as you make changes.

To access slots further down or to the right in the layout you can hold and drag the layout or click on the scroll arrows at the top-right.
